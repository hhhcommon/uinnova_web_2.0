Smart Hospital Visualization Management Solution

IoT, 3D, visualization

uinnova Hospital 3D Visualization System is used by hospitals as a 'patients guide for hospital consultation' 3D visualization solution. Our system uses 3D visualization technology as our foundation and scenes built using our 3D Builder consisting of buildings, floors, rooms, devices and medical supplies.

We focus on hospital surveillance and guiding patients to doctors and consultations by integrating with surveillance systems, HIS systems, CIS systems and their data to create a 3D scene displaying surveillance footage, map guiding, medical management and similar functions.

We allow patients to better understand hospitals, familiarize hospital environments, limit consultation times and more quickly complete their treatment process.

Integrated Systems


Video Surveillance System
Hospital Information System (HIS)
Clinical Information System (CIS)

Solution Values

uinnova offers Hospitals a 3D system to quickly recreate real life hospitals as an application with low costs, extremely beneficial for patients to lower consultation times and helping patients more accurately find their needed consultation.

Medical Advice

Patients can more directly understand a hospitals condition, including medical department conditions, medical device conditions, information on doctors, to better help patients to understand and trust the hospital, lowering the amount of times a patient has to return

Medical Consultation

Patients can freely roam inside a 3D scene to understand a hospitals layout and its surroundings. Patients coming for the first time can quickly find their destination, reduce consultation times and reduce stranded patients around the hospital.

Navigation

Using the uinnova hospital system allows hospitals to increase their image, service standards, and help patients better understand a hospital abilities and better allow patients to make medical situations.
